# Hands-On Security Assessment & Lab Portfolio

Welcome to my security research portfolio. This repository serves as a practical archive of vulnerability assessments, penetration testing, and ethical hacking reports conducted across various intentionally vulnerable laboratory environments and industry-standard training applications.

> **Legal & Ethical Compliance Note:** All assessments documented here were executed strictly within isolated local environments, standardized training labs, and open-source vulnerable applications. No live production systems, corporate networks, or non-disclosed VDP/Bug Bounty targets are included, adhering strictly to ethical hacking guidelines and non-disclosure standards.
